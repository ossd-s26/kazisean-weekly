In the last few weeks, I have become more thankful and proud of my team for choosing to work on [oppia](https://www.oppia.org/). This project is not only very well documented but also aligned with our mission of contributing to projects that have a real impact on education. The last few weeks were hard, as getting familiar with such a huge codebase takes time. Whenever I am stuck on a coding issue, I go to their website to contribute to the project by translating their lessons. My whole group, all of whom are bilingual, is also doing this. This is not only super fun but also keeps us from burning out, as we enjoy doing the translations. <!--more--> 

During our group work these past few days, we were mostly focused on getting our PRs ready and helping each other get unstuck. There weren't many discussions, as we were all assigned our own issues or PRs to work on. Most of our discussions focused on getting our PRs working and asking if anyone in the group was free to look over PR comments before submitting (as some of us have already made PRs to the repo). The test cases were the most time-consuming part for all of us, as they took forever to run. But we are making progress and can't wait to present what we have been working on.
